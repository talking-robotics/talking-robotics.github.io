Are you passionate about robotics, AI, and everything in between? Here's your chance to make an impact! Talking Robotics is seeking enthusiastic organizers to join our team. Fill out the form by the deadline and become part of our growing global community.

Who We Are

Talking Robotics is a platform led by dedicated volunteers, hosting virtual seminars and podcasts to connect researchers with the wider community and promote dialogue and collaboration. Find out more about what we do here.

Who Are We Looking For?

Our team has included PhD students, industry researchers, early-career academics, as well as undergraduate and master's students. We welcome individuals at all career stages who share our commitment to our goals and are willing to contribute a few hours each month to this initiative, both asynchronously and through Zoom meetings.

Why Join Us?

  • Network & Exposure: Interact with leading robotics and AI researchers, including industry experts, experienced professors, and emerging members of academia. Help build a valuable library of talks and podcasts on YouTube and Spotify.
  • Sponsor Collaboration: Partner with top industry and academic organizations for sponsorship and partnership efforts that make our mission possible. We have had the opportunity to work with companies and initiatives including Semio, Animatas, and PAL Robotics in the past.
